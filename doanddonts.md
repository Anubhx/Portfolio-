# DESIGN RULES

DO

Use whitespace aggressively.

Use typography as the hero.

Show process.

Use metrics.

Use accessibility best practices.

Design mobile carefully.

Use subtle hover states.

Animate entrances softly.

Use actual work.

Use MDX for scalability.

Use semantic HTML.

Keep interactions purposeful.

Prioritize readability.

Show engineering credibility.

Make transitions feel luxurious.

Maintain rhythm.

Think in systems.

Think like a recruiter.

THINK:
"What question does this section answer?"

DON'T

Do not use Dribbble gradients.

Do not use glassmorphism.

Do not overuse neon.

Do not create a template portfolio.

Do not use stock photos.

Do not fake metrics.

Do not autoplay videos.

Do not use carousels.

Do not overload animations.

Do not make users guess.

Do not hide CTAs.

Do not create huge walls of text.

Do not sacrifice accessibility.

Do not sacrifice performance.

Do not copy Denisha.

Do not make case studies look like PDFs dumped online.

Do not use meaningless buzzwords.

Do not prioritize aesthetics over clarity.
